How to Use the EV3 Compiler

×

1. Pair your EV3: Make sure your EV3 is paired to your Chromebook/PC via Bluetooth settings first.

2. Connect: Click "Connect to EV3" and select your device from the popup list.

3. Build: Drag blocks from the toolbox onto the workspace.

4. Upload: Click "Upload Program" to compile your code and send it directly to the EV3 brick.

5. Save and Load: Click "Save Blocks" to download your current code and "Load Blocks" to select a saved file.

Note: If your robot doesn't stop between movements, make sure to add a Wait block after the Stop Motor block!
